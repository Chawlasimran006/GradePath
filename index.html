Why GradePath?

Many students struggle to estimate their semester results and figure out the CGPA they need for scholarships, internships, or graduation. GradePath solves these problems by offering simple, accurate academic tools — no heavy frameworks, no sign‑ups required. It loads instantly and works on any device.
